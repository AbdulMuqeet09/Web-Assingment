Favourite Cities


Lahore

Lahore is the second largest city in Pakistan after Karachi and 26th largest in the world, with a population of over 13 million. It is situated in the north-east of the country with River Ravi flowing north-west of the city. It is the capital of the province of Punjab, where it is the largest city.

Islamabad

Islamabad is the capital city of Pakistan. It is the country's ninth-most populous city, with a population of over 1.2 million people, and is federally administered by the Pakistani government as part of the Islamabad Capital Territory.

New York

New York City comprises 5 boroughs sitting where the Hudson River meets the Atlantic Ocean. At its core is Manhattan, a densely populated borough that’s among the world’s major commercial, financial and cultural centers. Its iconic sites include skyscrapers such as the Empire State Building and sprawling Central Park. Broadway theater is staged in neon-lit Times Square.

Favourite Tourist Spots


Santorini, Greece

Santorini, Greece: With its white-washed buildings and blue domed roofs, Santorini is one of the most picturesque islands in the world. Known for its stunning sunsets, black sand beaches, and crystal-clear waters, it's no wonder why it's a top tourist destination.

Bali, Indonesia

Bali is a tropical paradise with beautiful beaches, lush jungles, and stunning temples. It's known for its rich culture and spirituality, as well as its luxurious resorts and villas.

Paris, France

Paris, France: Known as the City of Love, Paris is famous for its romantic ambiance, iconic landmarks, and delicious cuisine. From the Eiffel Tower to the Louvre Museum, Paris is a must-visit destination for anyone who loves culture, art, and history.

New York City, USA

The city that never sleeps, New York City is a bustling metropolis with something for everyone. From the bright lights of Times Square to the greenery of Central Park, there's always something to do and see in this vibrant city.

Tokyo, Japan

Tokyo is a modern city with deep roots in tradition and culture. From its incredible food scene to its stunning architecture, Tokyo is a must-visit destination for anyone who loves art, fashion, and technology.

Cape Town, South Africa

Cape Town, South Africa: Known for its stunning scenery, Cape Town is a city with a rich history and culture. With Table Mountain as its backdrop, Cape Town is a popular destination for adventure-seekers, foodies, and nature-lovers alike.
